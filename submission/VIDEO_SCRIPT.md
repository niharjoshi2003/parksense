## Production tips (make it feel premium)
- **Record the demo at 1080p**, browser zoom ~100–110%, hide bookmarks bar, full-screen the dashboard.
- **Cursor:** move slowly and deliberately. Pause 1s after each click so viewers can follow.
- **Pacing:** record a bit MORE demo footage than you need; trim to match the VO.
- **Music:** pick one calm-but-driving track (low volume, ~15–20%). Canva → Audio → search "corporate tech" / "inspiring".
- **Captions:** turn on auto-captions in Canva for accessibility (and silent autoplay on YT).
- **Branding:** keep the dark background (#0B1020) and yellow accent (#FFC200) consistent with the app + deck.
- **Intro/outro card:** reuse the deck's title slide as a PNG for a matching look.

## How to assemble it in Canva (step by step)
1. **Create design** → *Video* (1920×1080).
2. **Record your demo first.** Use Canva's screen recorder, or record with OBS/Xbox Game Bar and upload the clip. Follow Scenes 4–9 above for what to click.
3. **Upload audio:** left panel → **Uploads** → upload `voiceover.wav`. Drag it onto the timeline. It becomes your master track — everything else lines up to it.
4. **Lay visuals over the audio:** place each screen-recording clip / screenshot on the timeline so it matches the matching `[TIME]` block in this script. Trim clips to fit; speed up slow cursor movement with **Edit → Playback speed**.
5. **Add text overlays:** use the bold overlays listed under each scene. Keep them short, dark background, **yellow (#FFC200)** accent.
6. **Add music:** **Elements → Audio** → search "inspiring corporate" / "tech ambient". Set its volume to ~15–20% so the voice stays clear (click the track → volume).
7. **Captions:** top menu → **Captions** → auto-generate, then quickly fix any wrong words.
8. **Intro/outro:** add the title card (Scene 1) and the thank-you card (Scene 10) with a quick fade.
9. **Export:** **Share → Download → MP4 (1080p)**. Then upload to YouTube.
