[0:00 - 0:15]  HOOK  (title card)
------------------------------------------------------------
So here's the thing.
Our cities lose so much time to traffic every day.
And honestly, a big reason nobody talks about is parking.
A few cars in the wrong spot can jam a whole road.
That's exactly what ParkSense fixes.

------------------------------------------------------------
[0:15 - 0:45]  THE PROBLEM  (problem card)
------------------------------------------------------------
Here's the problem.
Near markets, metros, and event areas, people park wherever -
and that chokes traffic into fewer lanes.
Right now, enforcement teams are basically guessing.
There's no live map showing which spots actually hurt the most.
So the same jams keep coming back, every single day.

------------------------------------------------------------
[0:45 - 1:10]  WHY WE BUILT IT  (insight card)
------------------------------------------------------------
And this is the idea that got me started.
Not every wrongly parked car is the same.
A car on a busy road at 6 PM? Big deal.
The same car on a quiet street at midnight? Not really.
So instead of counting cars, we measure the actual impact
on traffic. That's the whole heart of ParkSense.

------------------------------------------------------------
[1:10 - 1:35]  WHAT WE BUILT  (live dashboard)
------------------------------------------------------------
So this is the dashboard.
On the left, a live map of the city's parking hotspots.
Red means act now, amber means watch it, green means it's fine.
On the right, every zone is ranked -
so you instantly know where to go first.

------------------------------------------------------------
[1:35 - 2:05]  THE SCORE  (live dashboard)
------------------------------------------------------------
Each hotspot gets a Congestion Impact Score, zero to a hundred.
It looks at how many cars are parked illegally,
the time of day, how important the road is,
the risk of spilling into junctions,
and repeat offenders.
One honest number for how bad a spot really is.

------------------------------------------------------------
[2:05 - 2:30]  SITUATION REPORT  (click top zone)
------------------------------------------------------------
Let me click our top red zone.
And right away, it gives a plain-English report -
what's happening, how bad it is, which patrol to send.
No jargon. Just a clear next step.

------------------------------------------------------------
[2:30 - 2:50]  TRANSPARENCY  (show raw payload)
------------------------------------------------------------
And it's not a black box.
One click - "show raw payload" - and you see
exactly how every score was calculated.
Fully explainable.

------------------------------------------------------------
[2:50 - 3:20]  IT REACTS  (time slider)
------------------------------------------------------------
Now watch this - I'll move the time slider.
At 6 PM, the whole city goes red.
But move to midnight... and most zones turn green.
So it's not "always red" - it follows real demand,
hour by hour. That's what makes it trustworthy.

------------------------------------------------------------
[3:20 - 3:45]  DISPATCH  (dispatch)
------------------------------------------------------------
And this is my favourite part.
One click, and I dispatch a patrol.
It updates the priority and even estimates
the commuter-minutes we just saved.
That's the gap between spotting a problem and fixing it.

------------------------------------------------------------
[3:45 - 4:15]  WRAP UP  (outro card)
------------------------------------------------------------
Under the hood it's Python and FastAPI doing the scoring,
with a React and Leaflet dashboard.
Everything's reproducible and explainable.
That's ParkSense - turning everyday parking data
into smart enforcement, at the right place and time.
I'm [your name] - thanks so much for watching.
